VERIZON

Logo Design

Client: (Student Project)
Service: Logo Design
Skills: Illustrator

CONCEPT

The Origin Story

Verizon is a telecommunication company. The redesign is inspired by the origin of the company name deriving from the latin word “veritas”, meaning truth, and the word “horizon”. The name combination is formed to show the company’s integrity and passion.

GOAL

Breaching Generations

Modernize and bring more personality to the logo so it appeals to younger market and further distinct itself from competitors.

RESULT

A New Horizon

An updated logo that takes into consideration Verizon’s history and origin while maintaining its iconic red with a gradient effect.
