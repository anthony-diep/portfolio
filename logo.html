Verizon Logo

Concept

Verizon is a telecommunication company. The redesign is inspired by the origin of the company name deriving from the latin word “veritas”, meaning truth, and the word “horizon”. The name combination is formed to show the company’s integrity and passion.

Button

Goals

Modernize and bring more personality to the logo so it appeals to younger market and further distinct itself from competitors.

Button

Result

An updated logo that takes into consideration Verizon’s history and origin while maintaining its iconic red with a gradient effect.

City Logo

Concept

Kuala Lumpur is the capital of Malaysia, a country in Southeast Asia. The design concept were inspired by the tiger as a symbol of Malaysia’s monarch history and the royal family’s current resident in Kuala Lumpur. The city’s tropical weather and its collection of gardens were also considered.

Button

Goals

Create a logo that symbolizes the city’s cultural heritage but also a welcoming graphic to promote tourism.

Button

Result

A mark that symbolizes the city’s cultural and monarchy heritage while also reflects its modern development.
