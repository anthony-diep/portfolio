Loki App

UI Design

Client: (Student Project)
Service: Application Design
Skills: Illustrator / XD / Photoshop

CONCEPT

Lorem Ipsum

The idea behind Loki came from being an avid user of Shazam’s sound sampling function. How useful would it be to have an app that perform similarly but with images? Think about the times you had to search a broad term on Google images to find the location of an image you found. Loki was designed to reduce time and frustration while achieving similar or better result.

GOAL

Lorem

The technology of today’s smartphones and tablets put human just a tab away from a never-ending flow of information. I wanted to apply this notion in designing an app that is unique but yet so simple that it literally can be cycled through with a few tabs. A complex idea made so simple that it will be your grandpa’s favorite app.

RESULT

Lorem Ipsum

A user-friendly interface that aims to perform the intended tasks in the simplest of manner that still produce user engagement at a high rate.
