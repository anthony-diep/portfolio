CONCEPT

In the hotel industry the term “fact sheet” refers to a one-page printed sheet detailing the basic information of the hotel, such as location, room types, and amenities. It is blocks of text congested on one sheet of paper. This project was aimed to a more visually appealing presentation of the fact sheet and have it available online.

GOAL

Lorem Ipsum

Design an online version of the hotel’s “fact sheet” that allows users to quickly access those general information of the hotel without needing to search through the hotel’s website.

RESULT

Lorem Ipsum

Converted plain text information that was on a printed sheet to a simple interface that is accessible online with easy navigation and more visually appealing.
