HAWAIISURFRIDERS

Branding | Packaging | Website

theBRAND

The Hawaii Surfriders is a fictional professional basketball team affiliated with the National Basketball Association.

theGOAL

For this student identity project, my focus was to create a system that can both presents the characteristics of the team and develop a connection with its fans.

Medium: Illustrator | Photoshop | XD

CONCEPT

The style of sports team logos generally consists of strong type and vibrant colors. I wanted to not only illustrate that style in the logo but also express the personalities of the brand. I chose to identify the team with a surfboard rising at the top of a wave instead of a mascot. One of the team’s main emphasis is fan participation. I want to convey the sense of togetherness in which anyone can be riding that wave, and that the “Surfriders” identity is all of us.

- ADDITIONAL ASSETS -

PATTERNS

Patterns decisions were based on the themes of waves and basketball movements. The sense of energy is also another design element of the patterns.

TICKETS

The tickets for each game will feature a different player to add collector value for fans.

SHOPPING BAGS

Shopping bags from the arena’s store features the profile image of the players

VIP WELCOME PACKAGE

A drawer style box presented to VIPs containing exclusive team merchandise.

JERSEY

Taking a page from the team name, the away jersey’s teal flush honors the island’s rich surf heritage.

Inspired by the natural landscape of the island. The colors and patterns simulate the unison of land and sea.

Features the team’s vibrant color across the primarily black jersey, symbolizing the island’s geographical location as the bridge between east and west.

HOME COURT

Adopting the franchise’s signature colors palette to create an environment that matches the brand personality.

- WEBSITE -

A website with a modern UI design and a cohesive theme that reflects the Surfriders identity. It fulfills the purpose of providing general team information to fans. The engaging and clean layout distinct it between other NBA team sites.
