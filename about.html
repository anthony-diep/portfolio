Anthony Diep

My name is Anthony. I was born in Vietnam and came to the States when I was 10. I always had an interest in design, but never thought of persuing an education and much less making a career out of it. I decided to take the safe route and got a bachelor degree in Marketing from the University of Hawaii. It wasn't something I was passionate about so here I am telling you my life story of how I started this journey of obtaining an associates degree in New Media Arts from Kapiolani Community College.
